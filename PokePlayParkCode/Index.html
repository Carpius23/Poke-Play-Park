How to play the game?

Engage in gaming sessions with your buddies on a single device or invite them to an online showdown. In case your friends aren't around, don't worry, you can test your skills against numerous pokemon enthusiasts globally by seeking an adversary. Alternatively, you can play against the clock if you want to prove yourself by making better times each try.

  1. The first player who get three in a row vertically, horizontally or diagonally, is the winner.
  2. If your mate doesn't get any answer, you can steal their turn
  3. Theres no order to put your pokemon, you can choose any option in the grid
